Rainbow Roll

Description

We love to eat assorted fish

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Seaweed Paper
  • Assorted Fish
  • Crab Meat
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

    • Steps

    1. Cook Sushi Rice
    2. Press sushi rice onto seaweed paper
    3. Place sliced cucumber and crab meat approximately 1 in from bottom of seaweed paper
    4. Roll ingredients into a tube
    5. Place assorted fish and sliced avocado on top

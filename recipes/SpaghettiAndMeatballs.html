Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

A classic dish

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Water
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Frozen Meatballs
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Heat Pasta Sauce and Meatballs
  3. Drain Noodles
  4. Combine cooked noodles, meatballs and heated pasta sauce
  5. Top with parmesan as desired
